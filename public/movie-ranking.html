Trikshala's Top Rated Movies

My Top 3 Movies of all time.

10 things I hate about You

This movie gives you that warm nostalgic feeling. Its a perfect movie to watch on a weekend. It has everything you need in a romcom. Simple drama, good humor, and most importantly good banter between the mains. 10/10. Would recommend!!

Me Before You

This movie is a bit angsty. Perfect for watching on those kinda days. Loved the acting, the chemistry between the actors, and the simple yet tragic plot 9/10. A must Watch!!

Superbad

This movie is a humorous one. An amazing watch with your friends if you're a teen. Great humor, simple plot and most importantly it binds up everything really well. 8/10. A good watch!!
